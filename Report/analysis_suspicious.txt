EMAIL ANALYSIS – SUSPICIOUS EMAIL

Email Type: Suspicious
Risk Level: Medium

Subject:
Payroll Update Required

Suspicious Indicators Identified:

1. Unknown Domain
The email contains a non-official payroll website link:
http://employee-payroll-check[.]com

2. Urgent Request
The message pressures employees to act immediately regarding salary processing.

3. Lack of Verification
The sender identity is unclear and cannot be verified.

4. Potential Credential Harvesting
The provided link may redirect users to a fake login page.

5. Social Engineering Indicators
The email attempts to gain trust by pretending to be from HR.

Conclusion:
This email is classified as suspicious because it contains several warning signs commonly associated with phishing attempts.
